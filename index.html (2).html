Our Story

Crafting Nature's
Finest Stone
Since 2008

Founded by Mr. Subhash Gupta — a stone industry veteran since 1993 — Acme Stones Pvt Ltd has grown from a Jaipur atelier into one of India's most respected natural stone manufacturers and global exporters.

As pioneers of CNC stone processing in Rajasthan, we introduced the Flow CNC Waterjet machine from the USA alongside advanced CNC routers, polishing systems, and shot blasting technology — delivering unmatched precision at every scale.

Why Acme Stones

The Acme Stones Advantage

Advanced CNC Technology
Pioneers of CNC waterjet cutting in Rajasthan using Flow machines from the USA, enabling sub-millimetre precision in stone carving and intricate pattern work.
Premium Natural Materials
Sourcing the finest marble, limestone, sandstone, slate, and granite from premier quarries across India and internationally to ensure superior character and durability.
Global Export Standards
Export-certified quality with professional packaging, ISPM-15 compliance, and complete logistics support to 50+ countries across Europe, Middle East, Americas & Asia.
Bespoke Fabrication
Full custom fabrication from concept to delivery. We collaborate with architects and interior designers to create entirely unique stone solutions tailored to your vision.
Precision Craftsmanship
Decades of artisanal expertise combined with modern manufacturing technology ensure unmatched quality, consistency, and perfection in every product we ship.
Architectural Expertise
Deep understanding of architectural applications. We don't just supply stone — we provide complete surface design consultation, technical drawings, and installation support.
Sustainable Processing
Responsible stone processing with water recycling systems, minimal waste production, and eco-conscious manufacturing practices throughout our operations.
Innovative Design Solutions
Continuously developing new patterns, textures, and stone applications at the intersection of tradition and contemporary design innovation.
